Indigenous Cultural Tour Guide & Indigenous Craft Market Coordinator

Tourism and Hospitality

GreenScape Solutions, Ltd

234 Sunshine Drive, Cebu City

Indigenous Cultural Tour Guide & Indigenous Craft Market Coordinator is a reputable company based in the vibrant city of Cebu, Philippines. With a specialized focus on Tourism and Hospitality, the company is dedicated to promoting and preserving the rich indigenous culture of the region. They offer immersive cultural tours led by knowledgeable guides, providing visitors with an authentic and enlightening experience. Additionally, the company plays a crucial role in supporting local artisans by coordinating an indigenous craft market, where unique and traditional crafts are showcased and made accessible to a broader audience. Committed to sustainability and cultural appreciation, the company strives to create meaningful connections between travelers and the indigenous heritage of Cebu City.

Job Description :

We are seeking passionate individuals to join our team as Indigenous Cultural Tour Guides & Indigenous Craft Market Coordinators. As part of the Tourism and Hospitality sector, you will play a pivotal role in showcasing the rich cultural heritage of Cebu City to visitors from around the world. Your responsibilities will include leading immersive and informative cultural tours, ensuring visitors have meaningful experiences. Moreover, you will be involved in coordinating and managing the indigenous craft market, supporting local artisans and promoting their traditional crafts. This role requires excellent communication skills, cultural sensitivity, and a genuine passion for preserving and promoting indigenous cultures.

Qualification :

Proven experience in the tourism industry, cultural exchange programs, or craft market coordination.

Excellent communication skills in English and local languages/dialects.

Deep knowledge and understanding of Cebu City's indigenous culture, history, and traditions.

Cultural sensitivity and ability to interact respectfully with diverse groups of people.

Strong organizational skills to coordinate craft market events and manage tour schedules effectively.

Flexibility to work irregular hours and weekends, as tours and market events may occur during these times.

Enthusiasm and passion for promoting sustainable tourism practices and preserving cultural heritage.

Primary Responsibilities :

Conduct engaging and informative cultural tours, providing visitors with insights into indigenous traditions, history, and practices.

Foster positive interactions and connections between visitors and local communities, ensuring cultural sensitivity and respect.

Coordinate and manage the indigenous craft market, liaising with local artisans, and organizing market events.

Promote indigenous crafts and products to visitors, encouraging fair trade practices that support local communities.

Collaborate with the team to develop and implement sustainable tourism strategies that contribute to the preservation of indigenous culture and traditions.
