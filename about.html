About us

We at TrabaWho are dedicated to empowering Indigenous communities by providing a platform that connects talented individuals with job opportunities that align with their skills and aspirations. Our goal is to foster inclusive and equitable employment practices that recognize and celebrate the unique contributions of Indigenous people.

Our business is founded in 2023, we, a group of passionate and socially conscious students, understood the challenges faced by indigenous communities. We built this platform to bridge the gap by highlighting the rich diversity within Indigenous cultures, we aimed to challenge misconceptions and foster appreciation for the immense contributions Indigenous people make to society.

We seek to provide indigenous people in the Philippines with an equal opportunity to find good jobs that will improve their lives and the lives of their families. And, aim to create a world where indigenous people are able to reach their full potential and showcase their skills in many different fields.
